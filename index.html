BIGFOOT SIGHTINGS IN AMERICA

Class:
   

Description

This innovative visualization explores Bigfoot sightings in America, categorizing them into different classes (Class A, B, and C).

The map on the top left provides a geographical representation of sightings, with each state shaded based on the frequency of encounters. Use the checkboxes to filter sightings by classification and interact with the visualizations. The statistics section provides an overview of the total and filtered reports.

The map on the top right provides a representation of sightings using tiny foot steps, with each state shaded based on the overall frequency of encounters.

The word cloud on the bottom left visualizes the most frequently mentioned words in the sighting reports, offering additional insights. The word cloud and the top left map are linked. when you click any of the state, you will get corresponding word cloud.

Explore the map, word cloud, and descriptive elements to gain a comprehensive understanding of Bigfoot sightings in the United States.
